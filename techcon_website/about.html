History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a single-day event with 200 attendees has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences globally, attracting over 10,000 participants from around the world. Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of showcasing breakthrough innovations, from the early days of mobile computing to today's AI revolution.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the global technology community. We believe in fostering innovation through knowledge sharing, creating opportunities for collaboration, and empowering the next generation of tech leaders. TechCon is committed to promoting diversity in technology and ensuring that cutting-edge innovations are accessible and beneficial to all of society.
